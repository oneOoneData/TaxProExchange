The fastest way to waste money on AI is to shop by category instead of by job. Here's the toolkit organized the way you actually work — with one honest caveat: this space moves monthly, so confirm current features and pricing before you commit, and check every tool against your WISP before client data goes anywhere near it.

## General thinking and drafting (your second brain's engine)

ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are powerful, cheap, and *not* built for tax — which makes them perfect for drafting, research starting points, and client communication, always on de-identified inputs and always with your review. Use the business or enterprise tiers that give you data-retention controls.

**Cost:** $20–$30/month per seat. Worth every penny for a sole practitioner.

**Best for:** Drafting client emails, summarizing documents, brainstorming planning strategies, creating first drafts of engagement letters.

## Tax research

Tax-native tools move you from question to a *citable* answer faster — which matters when preparer review is on the line.

- **CoCounsel Tax** (Thomson Reuters) — AI legal and tax research assistant. Strong on citations. Good for complex research questions where you need a code section to stand on.
- **Blue J** — Predicts tax case outcomes using AI trained on court decisions. Useful for gray areas where "it depends" is the usual answer.
- **TaxGPT** — Quick answers to tax questions. Less authoritative than primary-source tools but fast for common scenarios.

**Rule:** Verify against primary source regardless. Every single time.

## Document handling and practice management

**TaxDome** centralizes workflow, client portal, and billing, with AI for document naming and organization. This is where a startling number of your non-billable hours quietly disappear.

**Cost:** ~$99–$199/month for a solo practitioner. Pays for itself in portal time saved.

## Planning and advisory

Tools such as **CPA Pilot**, **Black Ore**, and **Intuit Tax Advisor** help surface deductions, run projections, and produce client-ready explanations — the advisory work that pays better than compliance ever will.

**Best for:** Moving clients from "here's your return" to "here's your plan."

## Your prep engine

ProConnect, Drake, UltraTax, and CCH Axcess increasingly ship their own AI copilots. Learn what yours already includes before you buy yet another tool. The built-in copilot integrates deeper than any third-party tool and doesn't require a new security review.

## How to choose without the overwhelm

1. Pick the one job costing you the most hours *right now*.
2. Trial a single tool for it (most offer 30 days).
3. Measure hours saved over two weeks before you add anything else.
4. Security gate: if it touches client data, it has to fit your WISP and offer the right data terms. No exceptions.
